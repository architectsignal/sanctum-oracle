Step 6 – Procure the Items

Protection Items

  • Sea Salt — To cast a protective circle on the floor or threshold.
  • Athame (Ritual Knife) — For directing energy and tracing the circle’s boundary.
  • White Candles — Symbolize purity and act as beacons of protective light.
  • Black Tourmaline (or Obsidian) — A grounding crystal that absorbs negative energy.
  • Frankincense or Sage Incense — Smoke to cleanse the space and raise the vibration.
  • Protective Amulet or Pentacle — Worn or placed at cardinal points to reinforce the barrier.
  • Herbal Sachets (e.g. lavender, rosemary, hyssop) — Scatter or burn in the circle for added shielding.

Summoning Items

  • Conjuration Circle — Pre‑drawn on parchment or directly on the ground (salt).
  • Sigil or Seal — Placed at the center.
  • Ritual Staff — To channel your will and focus the invocation.
  • Chalice — For offerings.
  • Incense Burner — To carry your words up on scented smoke.
  • Bell or Chime — To mark the beginning/end of each summoning phase.
  • Invocation Script — A written text that you speak aloud.
